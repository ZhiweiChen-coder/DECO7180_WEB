EV Benefits & Myth‑Busters

Discover the truth about electric vehicles and learn about their real advantages.

Myth: "Batteries only last 5 years"

Fact: Modern batteries retain over 80% capacity after 8–10 years and can be recycled. Most manufacturers offer 8-year warranties.

Source: Battery University Research

Lower Operating Costs

On current Australian prices, an EV's energy cost per kilometre is roughly one‑third of a petrol car. Maintenance costs are also 40% lower.

Source: NRMA Cost Comparison

Myth: "EVs are too expensive"

Fact: When including fuel savings and incentives, many EVs are cheaper over their lifetime than comparable petrol cars.

Source: Electric Vehicle Council

Environmental Impact

EVs produce 40-60% fewer emissions over their lifetime, even when accounting for manufacturing and electricity generation.

Source: Climate Council Report

Myth: "Not enough charging stations"

Fact: Australia has over 2,000 public charging stations, with new ones being added weekly. Most charging happens at home.

Source: ChargePoint Network

Performance Benefits

Instant torque delivery means faster acceleration, while regenerative braking extends range and reduces brake wear.

Source: EV Performance Studies

Myth: "EVs are slow"

Fact: Many EVs can accelerate from 0-100km/h in under 5 seconds, thanks to instant torque delivery.

Source: Manufacturer Specifications

Health Benefits

Zero tailpipe emissions mean cleaner air in cities, reducing respiratory problems and improving public health.

Source: World Health Organization
